Non-Fungible Tokens

An NFT is a digital asset that represents physical objects such as art, music, in-game items, and videos. They are purchased and sold online, frequently using cryptocurrencies such as Ethereum or Bitcoin and they are generally encoded in the form of a digital token that lives on blockchain technology.

In a nutshell, NFT is like a physical collector's item but it only exists in the real world. Owning one means you have exclusive ownership rights to it.

Before starting your NFT spree, make sure to remember that similar to cryptocurrency it is volatile. One day it might be at its peak price the next day it drops too low.
